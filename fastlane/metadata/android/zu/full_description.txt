CAPod uhlelo lokusebenza oluhambisana ne-AirPods.

Izici:

* Izinga lebhethri lama-pod nama-case.
* Isimo sokushaja se-pods ne-case.
* Ulwazi olwengeziwe mayelana nokuxhumeka, imakrofoni, ne-case.
* Inga thola futhi ibonise wonke amadivayisi aseduze.
* Ukutholwa kwendlebe ngokudlala/ukuma isikhashana okuzenzakalelayo.
* Xhuma ifoni ne-AirPods ngokuzenzakalela.
* Khombisa i-popup uma i-case ivuliwe.

I-CAPod ayinazo izikhangiso. Ezinye izici zidinga ukuthenga ngaphakathi nohlelo.

Amadivayisi amaningi adumile e-AirPods ne-Beats ayasekelwa.
Uma idivayisi yakho ifana ne-AirPods kodwa ingakasekelwa, ngithumele i-imeyili emfushane.

Unombono omuhle wesici esisha? Xhumana!
